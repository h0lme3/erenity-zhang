E-renity Checklist
The aim of this checklist is help you understand “E-renity” pre launch.
  • Understand the aim of the project is to provide digital serenity
  • Understand the necessity of digital serenity for social and tech sectors
  • Be able to list counter examples if digital serenity fails in any social or tech sector, respectively
  • Understand how our programs work from our website
  • Know how we operate as a revolutionary project, from cultural, professional, political, technical perspective
  • Understand that confidentiality and cybersecurity protection within our organization is indispensable
  • List a series of consequences if one project member or employee doesn’t do well or do as required in confidentiality guidelines and cybersecurity protection
  • Remember the guidelines to certain familiarity
Requirement for project members:
  • Consistently contribute to the project
  • Actively participate our programs that are in trial or development stages (internal education)
  • Understand the guidelines of confidentiality and conform to standard cybersecurity protection (free and included in the membership)
