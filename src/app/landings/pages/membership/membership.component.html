What is a member of E-renity and
What can I get from the membership?

The following content is for anyone that is not donating or investing in the project, but rather have general interest in working with us on E-renity.

If you are commited as a "E-renity member" that works for and with us together on this project, that we would rather receive your commitment by seeing your signature on "Joinee Board" !

Generally, investors and VCs don't have to sign up "Joinee Board", but it is not a strict rule. You can be an investor but also would like to contribute!

Please do so with the understanding that monthly meeting is compulsory, and you will then receive a confirmation email for "special recruitment". Thus you don't have to pay for the membership fee- but rather formally a member of us.

By being a member

You have the full rights to access all membership benefits and more than that, you have the opportunities to network with other members in monthly greet-and-learn (GnL). Everybody is gonna:

  1. Have cyberdefense suite and cyberawareness
  2. Learn about my story, in 2 ways
    a) in person presentation
    b) blog posts, or at least one article
  3. Be addressed as “E-renity project members” instead of “my team”, “my group”, or “my coworkers”, vice versa between project members, you could say “another member, or just call somebody his or her name” (if you work on a part of the project together, of course you could call him or her a partner or a group member), but everyone belongs to the project itself, not belong to me
  4. Work entirely part time in the first 25% of the project, participate writing part of the work contract by themselves
  5. Apply for bonus project reward when there is one available
  6. Sign on the “Joinee Board” of web page

By confirming being a member, you are committed to one type of relationships from the following with E-renity :

  • Employee : you need to submit your resume and be interviewed in a way that is either meeting or a phone call, then you will be selected to work as a certain role and contracted with Yinteligentsia; you are obliged to follow all regulations instructed under Yinteligentsia
  • Volunteer: you are simply interested in some concepts or the entire project, but you are aware that you have some blind points or incapabilities so that you can't engage fully; welcome to join as a volunteer which is an unpaid position but after finishing the commited work, you will gain another level of understanding toward many things in the project
  • Investor and stakeholder: you want to experience how E-renity works directly, “end-to-end”, and witness how exactly we do our work and are interested in contents we mentioned such as post-covid business strategic planning and cybersecurity vaccine products, etc; you are required to submit your investment proposal with detailed action plan upon application to this member type.
  • Highly-qualified cybersecurity researcher: after viewing what we have to present and the white paper, you are convinced that E-renity is your way to go and you'd like to have full access to our database built specially for your research; you believe in our mission and are extremely interested in the tech part of the project.

Each of these member types has a different dashboard on our “E-renity project” website. However, the common membership exclusives include:

  • Free cyber-vaccine (knowledge package + application)
  • Free training materials: post-covid themed value rebuilding, career coaching, trust rebuilding in the business world and in personal relationships
  • Free newspaper brief delivered to your email periodically based on the most sensitive matter in today's fast changing digital age
  • Free groupchat support for digital disruption coping strategies - “LiveCyberHelp”

For type-specific membership exclusives, we will update this file as we progress. But the general aim is to help each profession achieve E-renity and the general theme is coping with post-covid digital disruptions.

I look forward to you joining our membership. Thank you for taking the time to know about us.
