Digital Economy in Post Covid During Cyber Disruptions

Before covid, the world would shift to digitization, but the pandemic accelerated the shift. If there were any doubts regarding the need or necessity of all the businesses to shift to online business, the pandemic has closed them. During covid, all the business was closed due to lockdown, curfews and shutdowns, which increased the need for digital business.
